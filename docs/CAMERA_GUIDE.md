# Camera Guide

## Phone Cameras

Modern phones are good enough for many captures.

Use the main camera, not ultra-wide, when possible.

Avoid digital zoom.

## DSLR / Mirrorless

Use:

- moderate aperture
- fast enough shutter speed
- fixed exposure
- fixed white balance

## Video

Video is easy, but individual frames may be blurry.

For first Real Mode validation, use photos instead.

## Photo Count

Suggested counts:

- small object: 40–80 photos
- medium object: 80–150 photos
- room: 150+ photos
